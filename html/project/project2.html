RecipeRemix

AI-powered recipe recommendations based on preferences, restrictions, and cuisine fusion.

Overview

RecipeRemix is an AI-driven website that generates personalised recipe suggestions by combining user dietary preferences, available ingredients, and a taste for cross-cultural cuisine mixing. Users answer a short onboarding flow about their restrictions and favourite cuisines, and the app surfaces recipes that blend traditions in unexpected but delicious ways — think Korean-Mexican fusion or Vietnamese-Italian mashups. The project explores how AI can make cooking more adventurous and accessible.
