UI/UX Application

DormMate

A mobile app designed to make dorm life easier for university students.

Overview

DormMate is a mobile application concept built around the real challenges of living in student dormitories — shared spaces, conflicting schedules, noise complaints, and lost maintenance requests. The app gives residents a central hub for chore scheduling, roommate communication, maintenance ticketing, and community events. The design focused on reducing friction for everyday dorm interactions so students can spend more time on what matters.
