Witch Caper

A 3D VR game where you play as a witch navigating enchanted environments.

Overview

Witch Caper is an immersive VR experience built in Unity where players step into the shoes of a young witch exploring a mysterious, spell-filled world. The game combines puzzle-solving with exploration mechanics, using VR hand-tracking to let players cast spells, interact with objects, and uncover hidden secrets. The project was a deep dive into 3D environment design, VR interaction patterns, and building an atmosphere that feels magical from the inside out.
